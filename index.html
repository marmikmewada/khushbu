About Me

With over 11 years of experience as a UX Designer, I specialize in crafting exceptional digital experiences for web and mobile applications. I collaborate closely with stakeholders and users to define requirements and design specifications, creating intuitive architectures, wireframes, and personas.

My expertise in scenarios, prototypes, and interactive designs enhances user engagement. My strong communication and teamwork skills thrive in dynamic environments, allowing me to innovate and solve problems effectively.

I excel at leveraging technology to address user needs across multiple projects simultaneously. Committed to accessibility standards like Section 508 and WCAG 2.0, I ensure inclusivity and usability in every solution. In summary, I am passionate about enriching user experiences through technology, navigating complex challenges with creativity and finesse.
